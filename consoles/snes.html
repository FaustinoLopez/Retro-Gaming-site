Console Dossier

Super Nintendo (SNES)

Nintendo | Launched November 21, 1990

SNES delivered a gold-standard 16-bit library with rich sprite art, unforgettable soundtracks, and long-running genre-defining franchises.

  • Generation: Fourth
  • Media: Cartridge
  • Strength: 2D precision + audio

Technical Snapshot

CPU
Ricoh 5A22 @ 3.58 MHz
Main RAM
128 KB
Audio RAM
64 KB
Graphics
Mode 7 scaling and rotation
Output
RF / Composite / RGB

Must-Play Set

  1. Super Mario World
  2. The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past
  3. Chrono Trigger
  4. Super Metroid
  5. Donkey Kong Country 2

Why It Hit Hard

SNES represented the peak of cartridge-era 2D polish. Developers squeezed cinematic scenes, tight controls, and advanced effects from limited resources.

Collector Notes

  • Watch for shell yellowing from UV exposure.
  • Confirm cartridge slots read cleanly after light contact cleaning.
  • Inspect controller cable strain near connector heads.
