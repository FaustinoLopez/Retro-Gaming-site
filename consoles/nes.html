Why It Hit Hard

NES gave developers stable design rules and gave players trusted quality marks. It became the blueprint for console publishing structure through the 80s and beyond.

Collector Notes

  • Test both front-loader and top-loader units for reliability.
  • Clean 72-pin connectors gently before replacing hardware.
  • Avoid over-inserting cartridges to reduce connector wear.
