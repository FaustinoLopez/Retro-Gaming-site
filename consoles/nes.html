Console Dossier

Nintendo Entertainment System (NES)

Nintendo | Launched July 15, 1983

NES rebuilt home-console confidence and defined movement design patterns that still shape platformers and action games.

  • Generation: Third
  • Media: Cartridge
  • Strength: Foundational design

Technical Snapshot

CPU
Ricoh 2A03 @ 1.79 MHz
Main RAM
2 KB
Resolution
256 x 240
Audio
5-channel PSG
Output
RF / Composite

Must-Play Set

  1. Super Mario Bros. 3
  2. The Legend of Zelda
  3. Mega Man 2
  4. Metroid
  5. Castlevania III: Dracula's Curse

Why It Hit Hard

NES gave developers stable design rules and gave players trusted quality marks. It became the blueprint for console publishing structure through the 80s and beyond.

Collector Notes

  • Test both front-loader and top-loader units differently for reliability.
  • Clean 72-pin connectors gently before replacing hardware.
  • Avoid over-inserting cartridges to reduce connector wear.
