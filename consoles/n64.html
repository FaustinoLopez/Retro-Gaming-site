Console Dossier

Nintendo 64 (N64)

Nintendo | Launched June 23, 1996

N64 made analog movement and local four-player sessions a core part of console culture, especially in platformers and competitive shooters.

  • Generation: Fifth
  • Media: Cartridge
  • Strength: Couch multiplayer

Technical Snapshot

CPU
NEC VR4300 @ 93.75 MHz
Main RAM
4 MB (8 MB with Expansion Pak)
Graphics
SGI-derived Reality Co-Processor
Controllers
Analog stick + accessory slot
Output
Composite / S-Video / RGB mod

Must-Play Set

  1. Super Mario 64
  2. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
  3. GoldenEye 007
  4. Mario Kart 64
  5. Super Smash Bros.

Why It Hit Hard

N64 crystallized 3D camera language and analog input logic. Many modern adventure and shooter control conventions trace directly back to this generation.

Collector Notes

  • Original sticks often loosen; replacement modules vary in precision.
  • Expansion Pak doors are easy to lose; keep originals safe.
  • Confirm AV cable and power brick compatibility by region.
