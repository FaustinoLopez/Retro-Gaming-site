Game Boy Color took the original handheld formula and added color, speed, and personality while keeping strong backward compatibility.

Why It Hit Hard

GBC proved that handheld systems could evolve quickly without losing install base. It extended portable play into a full-color mainstream social habit.

Collector Notes

  • Check battery terminals for corrosion and clean early.
  • Inspect speaker output for static from aging caps.
  • Screen lens scratches are common and usually replaceable.
